About

Two bikes and two curious spirits on the journey through the beautiful landscapes in South America. Starting off in Sao Paolo (Brasil) on October the 13th, 2016 we (Simon and Julia) begin our bike tour towards Patagonia and la Tierra del Fuego. On this blog you can follow us on our trip and travel stops. If you think now: Where exactly do they plan to going? How long will this trip be? ...then you are wondering exactly the same as we do. We don't know where this journey will bring us but we will be glad to share our pictures and experiences with you! Hope you enjoy!
